Under the hood

Small app.
Serious output.

3x Upscale Pipeline

Images are upscaled with Lanczos3 filtering before tracing, preserving fine details and sharp text that other tools lose.

Native Desktop App

Built with Tauri and Rust. Starts instantly, uses minimal memory, and runs entirely on your machine — no Electron bloat.

Optimized Tracing

Hand-tuned potrace parameters balance smooth curves with sharp corners — optimized for logos, icons, and text.

Instant Clipboard

One click or Cmd+C copies clean SVG markup. Paste directly into Figma, Paper, Sketch, or your code editor.
